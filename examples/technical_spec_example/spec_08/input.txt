TECHNICAL REFERENCE — AQUASHIELD AWS-300 WATER QUALITY ANALYZER

Manufacturer: EnviroSense Analytics
Part Number: ESA-AWS300-PRO
Document Rev: 3.1
Date: 2024-07-20

The AWS-300 is a multi-parameter WQA (Water Quality Analyzer) designed for continuous online monitoring in WTP (Water Treatment Plant) and WWTP (Wastewater Treatment Plant) applications. It supports SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) integration and meets EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) Method compliance.

Measurement Parameters:
- pH: Range 0–14, resolution 0.01, accuracy ±0.02, ISE (Ion-Selective Electrode) sensor
- ORP (Oxidation-Reduction Potential): -2000 to +2000 mV
- DO (Dissolved Oxygen): 0–20 mg/L, LDO (Luminescent Dissolved Oxygen) technology
- Turbidity: 0–4000 NTU (Nephelometric Turbidity Units), EPA 180.1 compliant
- Conductivity: 0–200 mS/cm, TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) calculated
- Chlorine (free): 0–20 mg/L, DPD (N,N-diethyl-p-phenylenediamine) colorimetric method
- TOC (Total Organic Carbon): 0–25 mg/L, UV254 (Ultraviolet absorbance at 254nm) method
- TSS (Total Suspended Solids): 0–50 g/L, NIR (Near-Infrared) scatter

Communication & Control:
- Protocols: Modbus TCP/IP, HART (Highway Addressable Remote Transducer), 4-20mA analog outputs
- I/O: 8x DI (Digital Input), 4x DO (Digital Output), 4x AI (Analog Input) 4-20mA
- HMI (Human-Machine Interface): 7" color TFT touchscreen
- Data Logging: 1M records, CSV export via USB or FTP (File Transfer Protocol)
- Power: 100–240 VAC, 50/60 Hz, 85W max
- Enclosure: NEMA 4X / IP66, 316L SS (Stainless Steel) housing
- Certifications: NSF/ANSI 61, MCERTS (Monitoring Certification Scheme)

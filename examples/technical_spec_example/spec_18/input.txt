SPECIFICATION — CRYOSTAT CS-4500 SUPERCONDUCTING MAGNET SYSTEM

Manufacturer: PhysTech Cryogenics
Model: CS-4500-HTS
Document ID: PTC-SPEC-2024-019
Date: 2024-05-18

The CS-4500 is a dry (cryocooler-based) superconducting magnet system using HTS (High-Temperature Superconductor) REBCO (Rare Earth Barium Copper Oxide) tape. It is designed for NMR (Nuclear Magnetic Resonance), EPR (Electron Paramagnetic Resonance), and particle beam steering applications.

Magnet Specifications:
- Central Field: 12.0 T (Tesla)
- Bore Diameter: 52mm warm bore, 100mm cold bore
- Homogeneity: 1 ppm (parts per million) DSV (Diameter Spherical Volume) = 10mm
- Temporal Stability: < 0.01 ppm/hr in persistent mode
- Conductor: REBCO tape, 77K Ic (Critical Current) = 600 A, width 12mm
- Operating Current: 180 A
- Inductance: 85 H (Henrys)
- Stored Energy: 1.38 MJ (Megajoules)
- Ramp Rate: 0.1 A/s to 0.5 A/s (quench protection limited)

Cryogenics:
- Cooling: Two-stage GM (Gifford-McMahon) cryocooler, 1.5W @ 4.2K
- 1st Stage: 45W @ 45K (thermal shield)
- 2nd Stage: 1.5W @ 4.2K (magnet)
- Cool-down Time: < 18 hours from RT (Room Temperature) to 4.2K
- LHe (Liquid Helium): Not required (cryogen-free operation)
- Thermal Shield: MLI (Multi-Layer Insulation) + copper radiation shield

Safety & Protection:
- Quench Detection: dV/dt bridge circuit, threshold 100 mV
- Quench Protection: Active dump resistor, 0.5 Ω, energy dump < 30 sec
- Emergency: QDS (Quench Detection System) with automatic heater activation
- Stray Field: 5 Gauss line at 2.5m radial from center
- Compliance: IEC 61010-1 (safety), NFPA 55 (compressed gases), ASME BPVC (Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code) Section VIII

Utilities:
- Power Supply: 200A / 10V bipolar PSU, stability ±10 ppm
- Compressor: Water-cooled, 7 kW electrical input, 8 L/min cooling water
- Control: LabVIEW-based GUI, GPIB (General Purpose Interface Bus) + Ethernet
- Dimensions: 800mm (D) x 800mm (W) x 1200mm (H)
- Weight: 650 kg (magnet + cryostat)

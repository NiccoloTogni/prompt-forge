ENGINEERING SPECIFICATION — AEROCORE ACM-550 FLIGHT CONTROLLER

Developer: SkyDynamics Aerospace
Part ID: SDA-ACM550-MIL
Spec Revision: D4
Effective: 2024-01-18

The ACM-550 is a ruggedized FCU (Flight Control Unit) for UAV (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) platforms operating in BVLOS (Beyond Visual Line of Sight) missions. It integrates triple-redundant IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit) and dual GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) receivers.

Performance Specifications:
- IMU: 3x MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) 9-DOF (Degrees of Freedom) sensors
- GNSS: Dual-band L1/L5, RTK (Real-Time Kinematic) capable, CEP (Circular Error Probable) < 2cm
- AHRS (Attitude and Heading Reference System): Fusion rate 400 Hz
- Autopilot Modes: RTL (Return to Launch), WPT (Waypoint) nav, ORBIT, VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) transition
- Interfaces: 4x UART, 2x SPI, 2x I2C, 1x CAN FD (Flexible Data-rate)
- PWM Outputs: 14 channels for ESC (Electronic Speed Controller) and servo control
- Telemetry: MAVLink v2 over 900 MHz ISM (Industrial, Scientific, Medical) band
- Processor: STM32H7 dual-core @ 480 MHz with FPU (Floating-Point Unit)
- Memory: 2MB flash, 1MB SRAM
- Supply Voltage: 4.5V–6.0V regulated via onboard BEC (Battery Eliminator Circuit)
- Vibration: MIL-STD-810G Method 514.7
- Operating Temp: -20°C to +70°C
- Weight: 48g (without enclosure)
- Compliance: DO-160G (environmental), STANAG 4586 (interoperability)

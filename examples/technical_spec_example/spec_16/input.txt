PRODUCT SPECIFICATION — IONBEAM IBX-350 MASS SPECTROMETER

Manufacturer: AnalytiChem Instruments
Model: IBX-350-QQQ
Part Number: ACI-IBX350-V2
Date: 2024-07-08

The IBX-350 is a QQQ (Triple Quadrupole) mass spectrometer with ESI (Electrospray Ionization) and APCI (Atmospheric Pressure Chemical Ionization) sources. It is designed for quantitative LC-MS/MS (Liquid Chromatography Tandem Mass Spectrometry) analysis in clinical, forensic, and environmental testing laboratories.

Analytical Performance:
- Mass Range: m/z 5–3000 (mass-to-charge ratio)
- Resolution: Unit mass resolution across full range (FWHM, Full Width at Half Maximum)
- Scan Speed: 15,000 Da/s (Daltons per second)
- MRM (Multiple Reaction Monitoring) transitions: 512 simultaneous
- Polarity Switching: < 15 ms
- Sensitivity (ESI+): 500 fg (femtograms) reserpine, S/N > 100:1 in MRM mode
- Dynamic Range: 6 orders of magnitude (1E1 to 1E6)
- Mass Accuracy: ±0.1 Da
- CID (Collision-Induced Dissociation): 0–180 eV, N2 (nitrogen) collision gas

Ion Source:
- ESI Spray Voltage: ±5.5 kV
- APCI Corona Current: 0–10 µA
- Source Temperature: RT–650°C (ESI); RT–500°C (APCI)
- Nebulizer Gas: N2 @ 0–80 psi (pounds per square inch)
- Desolvation Gas: N2 @ 200–1200 L/hr

System:
- Vacuum: Two-stage differential pumping, TMP (Turbomolecular Pump) 300 L/s
- Detector: EM (Electron Multiplier), gain 1E6–1E7
- Data System: ChromaLab DAS v5.1, CFR 21 Part 11 compliant (Code of Federal Regulations)
- Interface: USB 3.0, LAN, RS-232 auxiliary
- LC Compatibility: UHPLC (Ultra-High Performance Liquid Chromatography) up to 1500 bar
- Power: 200–240 VAC, 50/60 Hz, 2.5 kVA
- Gas Requirements: N2 generator or cylinder supply, 99.5% purity
- Dimensions: 710mm (W) x 620mm (D) x 580mm (H)
- Weight: 145 kg
- Regulatory: CE-IVD (In Vitro Diagnostic), FDA 21 CFR Part 11, ISO 13485 (quality management for medical devices)

COMPONENT DATASHEET — NANOFLUX NFC-120 RF TRANSCEIVER IC

Manufacturer: WaveTech Microelectronics
Part Number: WT-NFC120-QFN48
Process Node: 22nm FD-SOI (Fully Depleted Silicon on Insulator)
Date: 2024-10-01

The NFC-120 is a highly integrated RF (Radio Frequency) transceiver SoC (System on Chip) for LPWAN (Low-Power Wide-Area Network) applications including LoRa (Long Range), NB-IoT (Narrowband Internet of Things), and LTE-M (Long Term Evolution for Machines).

RF Specifications:
- Frequency Bands: Sub-GHz (410–510 MHz, 862–960 MHz), LTE B1/B3/B8/B20
- TX (Transmit) Power: +22 dBm (sub-GHz), +23 dBm (LTE)
- RX (Receive) Sensitivity: -148 dBm (LoRa SF12 BW125), -107 dBm (NB-IoT)
- NF (Noise Figure): 5.5 dB
- ADC (Analog-to-Digital Converter): 12-bit, 40 MSPS (Mega Samples Per Second)
- DAC (Digital-to-Analog Converter): 12-bit, 80 MSPS
- Phase Noise: -130 dBc/Hz @ 1 MHz offset
- EVM (Error Vector Magnitude): < 2.5% for 16-QAM (Quadrature Amplitude Modulation)

Digital & System:
- Core: ARM Cortex-M33 with TrustZone, DSP (Digital Signal Processing) extensions
- Memory: 512KB flash, 256KB SRAM, 8KB OTP (One-Time Programmable)
- Peripherals: SPI, I2C, 3x UART, 1x SDIO (Secure Digital Input Output), GPIO (General Purpose Input Output)
- Security: AES-256 (Advanced Encryption Standard), ECDSA (Elliptic Curve Digital Signature Algorithm), secure boot, RoT (Root of Trust)
- Power: 4.8 mA RX, 120 mA TX @ +22 dBm, 1.2 µA deep sleep (RTC on)
- Supply: 1.8V–3.6V
- Package: QFN-48 (Quad Flat No-lead), 6mm x 6mm, 0.4mm pitch
- Temperature: -40°C to +105°C (industrial grade)
- Compliance: 3GPP Rel. 14 (NB-IoT/LTE-M), ETSI EN 300 220, FCC Part 15.247

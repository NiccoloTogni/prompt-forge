TECHNICAL BULLETIN — CRYOZEN CZR-1200 REFRIGERATION COMPRESSOR

Manufacturer: ThermalWorks Industrial
Catalog No.: TWI-CZR1200-HE
Issue: TB-2024-047
Date: 2024-05-02

The CZR-1200 is a high-efficiency VSD (Variable Speed Drive) scroll compressor for HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) and process cooling in cleanroom and pharmaceutical environments.

Specifications:
- Refrigerant: R-513A (GWP 631), A2L low-flammability classification per ASHRAE 34
- Cooling Capacity: 120 kW @ AHRI (Air-Conditioning, Heating, and Refrigeration Institute) rated conditions
- COP (Coefficient of Performance): 4.2 at full load; IPLV (Integrated Part Load Value): 6.8
- Speed Range: 1800–7200 RPM via VFD (Variable Frequency Drive)
- Motor: IPM (Interior Permanent Magnet) synchronous, IE5 efficiency class
- Oil System: POE (Polyol Ester) oil, OCR (Oil Circulation Rate) < 1.5%
- Discharge Pressure: max 32 bar(g)
- Suction Pressure: 2–10 bar(g)
- Sound Level: 72 dB(A) @ 1m per ISO 3744
- Electrical Supply: 400V 3ph 50Hz, PF (Power Factor) > 0.95 with onboard PFC (Power Factor Correction)
- Starting Current: < 2A (soft start via VFD)
- Vibration: < 2.5 mm/s RMS per ISO 10816-1
- Safety: PED (Pressure Equipment Directive) 2014/68/EU, ATEX Zone 2 (optional)
- Dimensions: 850mm (L) x 600mm (W) x 720mm (H)
- Weight: 185 kg dry

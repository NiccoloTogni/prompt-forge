SPECIFICATION DOCUMENT — HELIOGEN HLG-600 CONCENTRATED SOLAR THERMAL COLLECTOR

Manufacturer: SolarCore Thermal Systems
Model: HLG-600-PT
Engineering Doc: SC-ENG-2024-031
Date: 2024-03-15

The HLG-600 is a parabolic trough CSP (Concentrated Solar Power) collector designed for IPP (Independent Power Producer) and industrial process heat (IPH) applications. It uses a novel ETC (Evacuated Tube Collector) receiver with selective coating for high absorptance.

Optical & Thermal:
- Aperture Width: 6.0m
- Module Length: 12.0m per SCA (Solar Collector Assembly)
- Collector Area: 72 m² per SCA
- Concentration Ratio: 80:1
- Peak Optical Efficiency: 77% (IAM-corrected, incidence angle modifier)
- Absorptance: 96.5% per ASTM E903
- Emittance: 8.5% @ 400°C per ASTM E408
- HTF (Heat Transfer Fluid): VP-1 (biphenyl/diphenyl oxide) or molten salt (60% NaNO3, 40% KNO3)
- Operating Temperature: 290°C–550°C (VP-1), up to 565°C (molten salt)
- Thermal Output: 430 kWth (kilowatts thermal) per SCA at DNI (Direct Normal Irradiance) = 900 W/m²

Structural & Tracking:
- Mirror Material: Low-iron float glass, 4mm, reflectance > 94% (per ISO 9050)
- Tracking: Single-axis SPA (Sun Position Algorithm) based, accuracy ±0.05°
- Drive: Hydraulic linear actuator with LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) feedback
- Wind Rating: Operational up to 14 m/s; stow at 25 m/s
- Foundation: Concrete pylons, designed per ACI 318 (American Concrete Institute)
- Seismic: Zone 4 per IBC (International Building Code)

System Integration:
- Controller: PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) based, IEC 61131-3 compliant
- Communication: OPC UA (Open Platform Communications Unified Architecture), Modbus TCP
- Monitoring: Pyrheliometer for DNI, RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) Pt100 sensors
- Design Life: 30 years per NREL SAM (System Advisor Model) analysis
- Certifications: EN 12975 (thermal solar collectors), IEC 62862 (CSP)
- Country of Origin: USA, ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations) free

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION — AETHON AT-2200 AUTONOMOUS MOBILE ROBOT

Manufacturer: FleetMind Robotics
Model: AT-2200-WH
HW Revision: Rev E
Date: 2024-11-15

The AT-2200 is an AMR (Autonomous Mobile Robot) designed for intralogistics in warehouse and 3PL (Third-Party Logistics) environments. It uses SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) with sensor fusion for navigation in dynamic spaces without fixed infrastructure.

Navigation & Sensing:
- SLAM: Visual-Inertial SLAM using stereo cameras + IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit)
- LIDAR: 2D RPLidar S2, 360° scan, 30m range, 32K points/sec
- Depth Camera: Intel RealSense D455, range 0.4–6m, RGB-D (Red Green Blue - Depth)
- Obstacle Detection: 3D TOF (Time-of-Flight) arrays, front and rear, 0.1–4m range
- Odometry: Dual wheel encoders + visual odometry (VO)
- Localization Accuracy: ±10mm repeatable (RMS)

Motion & Payload:
- Payload Capacity: 2,200 kg
- Max Speed: 2.0 m/s loaded, 2.5 m/s unloaded
- Turning Radius: 0m (differential drive, in-place rotation)
- Ramp Capability: Up to 5° incline loaded
- Lift Mechanism: Integrated scissor lift, 250mm stroke

Compute & Communication:
- SBC (Single Board Computer): NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin, 275 TOPS AI performance
- Middleware: ROS2 Humble on Ubuntu 22.04 LTS
- Fleet Management: Proprietary FMS (Fleet Management System), VDA 5050 compliant
- Communication: Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax 6GHz), 5G NR (New Radio) module optional
- API: REST + WebSocket for WMS (Warehouse Management System) integration
- Safety: PLd/Cat.3 per ISO 13849, certified per ISO 3691-4 (driverless trucks)
- E-stop: Dual-channel SIL 2 (Safety Integrity Level) rated

Power:
- Battery: 48V 60Ah LFP, 2.88 kWh
- Charging: Auto-docking, opportunity charging, 0–80% in 45 min
- Runtime: 8–12 hours depending on load profile
- BMS: Integrated with CAN bus telemetry
- Dimensions: 1400mm (L) x 850mm (W) x 350mm (H, lowered)
- Weight: 380 kg (without payload)

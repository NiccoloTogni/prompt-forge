CHILLPRO CHP-150 PROCESS CHILLER — DATA SHEET

Mfr: ThermoFlow Cooling Solutions LLC
Item Number: TFC-CHP150-AW
Manufacture Date: 2023

Power:
460V 3-phase 60Hz
Connected load: 55 kW

Operating weight: 3,950 lbs

The unit is designed for indoor or outdoor (with rain canopy) use.
Operating ambient: 14 °F to 113 °F.
Humidity tolerance: up to 95% RH.

Performance:
Compressor speed: 3,500 RPM
Water flow rate: 25.8 m³/h
Noise level: 78 dB(A) at 1m

Regulatory: CE, UL 474, ASHRAE 15 (refrigeration safety), PED 2014/68/EU

Communication: BACnet IP, Modbus RTU

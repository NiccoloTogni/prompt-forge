WeldMaster WM-R400 Robotic Welding Cell
Product Information Sheet

Integrator: ArcFusion Welding Systems
Part #: AFS-WMR400-MIG
Year: 2024

The WM-R400 is a fully enclosed robotic MIG/MAG welding cell featuring a 6-axis welding robot with 2-axis headstock/tailstock positioner. It is designed for structural steel welding in the construction and heavy equipment sectors.

Power:
Input 480V AC, 3 phases, 60 Hz. Total installed power including welding source: 85 kW.

Physical Data:
Cell footprint (L×W): 4.5 m × 3.2 m
Cell height: 2.8 m
Weight: approx. 5.5 tons (complete cell)

Environment:
Working temperature: 0 °C to 40 °C
Humidity: up to 90% non-condensing

Performance:
Robot max speed: 2,000 mm/s (TCP)
Duty cycle throughput: 18 assemblies per shift (8h)
Weld seam accuracy: ±0.3 mm (after TCP calibration)
Sound level: 80 dB(A) with enclosure doors closed

Safety: CE, ISO 10218-2 (robot system integration), EN ISO 11611 (welding environment)

Interfaces: EtherNet/IP to cell PLC, PROFINET to plant SCADA

Service interval: 3,000 hours for robot, 1,000 hours for torch consumables
Standard warranty: 2 years

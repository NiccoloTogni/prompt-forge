BendForce BF-320 CNC Press Brake
Technical Specification Document

Manufacturer: MetalForm Machinery NV
Code: MFM-BF320-CNC6
Year: 2024

Description:
The BF-320 is a 320-tonne CNC hydraulic press brake with 6-axis backgauge for precision sheet metal bending. It serves the general sheet metal fabrication industry.

Bending length: 4,100 mm
Bending force: 3,200 kN

Electrical:
  - Mains: 400V, 3-ph, 50 Hz
  - Total power: 30 kW

Machine envelope:
  4.6 m long × 1.8 m wide × 2.9 m tall
  Mass: 14,800 kg

Working conditions:
  Ambient temperature from 5 to 45°C. Max humidity 80%.

Bending performance:
  Ram speed: Approach 200 mm/s, bending 10 mm/s, return 180 mm/s
  Throughput: varies by part complexity
  Backgauge accuracy: ±0.02 mm
  Noise during bending: 76 dB(A)

Compliance: CE, EN 12622 (press brake safety), ISO 13849-1 PLd
Interfaces: PROFINET, USB, Ethernet (for remote support)
Service: 3,000 hours between scheduled services
Warranty: 2 years

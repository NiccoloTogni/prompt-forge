TECHNICAL DATA — CONVEYPRO CP-1800 BELT CONVEYOR SYSTEM

Supplier: TransLine Material Handling
Ref: TLM-CP1800-HD
Manufactured: 2023

APPLICATION
The CP-1800 is a heavy-duty belt conveyor designed for aggregate and bulk material transport in the mining and quarry sector. Belt width is 1800 mm with a design capacity of 2,500 t/h.

POWER
Voltage: 690 V AC
3-phase / 50 Hz
Drive motor rating: 250 kW (2× 125 kW geared motors)

DIMENSIONS
The conveyor frame is 24 m long, 2.4 m wide, and 1.6 m high (at discharge end).
Total installed weight: approximately 14,200 kg.

ENVIRONMENT
Rated for outdoor use in temperatures from –20 °C to +50 °C.

OPERATIONAL PARAMETERS
Belt speed (max): 3.5 m/s
Throughput: 2,500 tonnes per hour
Noise: 82 dB(A) measured at 2 meters from drive station

STANDARDS
CE marking, EN 620 (continuous handling equipment), ISO 5048

INTERFACES
Hardwired I/O + Modbus RTU for PLC integration

MAINTENANCE
Lubrication and inspection recommended every 2,000 operating hours.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATION
======================

Product: SPINMAX CL-600 CNC Turning Center
Made by: KronoWerk Precision Machines GmbH
Catalog Reference: KWP-CL600-R3
Production Year: 2024

DESCRIPTION
The SPINMAX CL-600 is a slant-bed CNC lathe with live tooling, primarily used in aerospace component manufacturing. It features a 12-station turret with driven tools and a bar feeder interface.

ELECTRICAL REQUIREMENTS
Mains voltage is 380V three-phase at 50 Hz. Total connected load amounts to 37 kW.

MACHINE ENVELOPE
Length: 2.8 m | Width: 1.75 m | Height: 1.95 m
Shipping weight approximately 6200 kg.

ENVIRONMENTAL LIMITS
The machine should be operated between 10°C and 40°C. Humidity must stay below 75% (non-condensing).

KEY PERFORMANCE DATA
- Spindle max speed: 4500 RPM
- Production rate: 45 parts/hour
- Positional accuracy: 0.008 mm
- Acoustic emission: 72 dB(A)

The CL-600 is CE marked and conforms to ISO 13849-1 (Performance Level c) and EN 12415 (safety of turning machines).

Interfaces available: PROFINET and MTConnect.

Regular preventive maintenance is advised every 3000 hours of operation. Standard warranty covers 2 years from date of commissioning.

LaserTech LT-6025 Fiber Laser Cutting System

MANUFACTURER: PhotonCut Industrial Lasers S.p.A.
ARTICLE NUMBER: PCI-LT6025-F8
YEAR: 2024

Overview: The LT-6025 is a flat-bed fiber laser cutter with 8 kW resonator, engineered for high-speed sheet metal processing in the HVAC and elevator panel fabrication sector. Cutting area is 6,000 × 2,500 mm.

Electrical Data:
  - Voltage: 400 V, three-phase, 50 Hz
  - Consumption: 45 kW average, 68 kW peak

Machine Dimensions:
  Length = 11.2 m, Width = 3.8 m, Height = 2.15 m
Weight: 13,600 kg

Environment:
  Temp range: 41°F – 95°F
  Humidity: ≤70% non-condensing

Performance:
  Rapid traverse: 140 m/min (simultaneous XY)
  Cutting speed (1mm mild steel): 40 m/min
  Throughput: up to 180 parts/hour (application dependent)
  Positioning accuracy: ±0.03 mm
  Noise: 75 dB(A) during cutting

Standards & Approvals:
  CE, EN ISO 11553-1 (laser safety), IEC 60825-1 Class 4

Interfaces: PROFINET, OPC UA, USB (for offline programs)

Maintenance: 2,500 hours for resonator service, 1,000 hours for general inspection.
Warranty: 2 years machine, 3 years laser source.

PRODUCT DATA: PYROMAX PM-1200 INDUSTRIAL BATCH FURNACE

Made by HeatTech Thermal Equipment
Ident: HTE-PM1200-EL
Year of production: 2023

The PM-1200 is an electrically heated batch furnace used for heat treatment processes (hardening, tempering, annealing) in the automotive and aerospace heat treatment sector. Maximum operating temperature is 1,200 °C.

ELECTRICAL
Supply: 400 V / 3-phase / 50 Hz
Heating elements: 120 kW total (6 × 20 kW SiC rod elements)

DIMENSIONS
External: 3,500 mm (L) × 2,200 mm (W) × 2,800 mm (H)
Weight: 6,500 kg

OPERATIONAL ENVIRONMENT
Installed in climate-controlled facility: 10°C to 40°C ambient. Humidity below 75%.

OUTPUT DATA
Heating rate: 300 °C/hour (loaded)
Temperature uniformity: ±5 °C within working zone (per AMS 2750F Class 2)
Cycle throughput: 3 loads/day (depending on recipe)
Noise: 55 dB(A) — primarily from cooling fans

CERTIFICATIONS
CE, EN 746-1 (industrial thermoprocessing equipment), AMS 2750F (pyrometry), NADCAP accredited process

MAINTENANCE: Every 4,000 hours (element inspection, thermocouple recalibration)
WARRANTY: 3 years on chamber, 1 year on heating elements

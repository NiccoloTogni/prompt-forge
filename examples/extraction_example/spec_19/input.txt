SPECIFICATION — LOGIMOVE LM-1500 AUTOMATED GUIDED VEHICLE

Manufacturer: SmartTrans Logistics Automation
Order number: SLA-LM1500-NAV
Production year: 2024

Overview
The LM-1500 is a laser-guided AGV (Automated Guided Vehicle) with a 1,500 kg payload for pallet transport in the warehouse and intralogistics sector. It uses natural feature navigation (SLAM) with safety-rated laser scanners.

Electrical
Battery: 48V / 200Ah LFP (charges from 400V 3-phase 50Hz mains via onboard charger)
Drive power: 4.8 kW (2× 2.4 kW wheel motors)

Physical
Length: 1,600 mm
Width: 900 mm
Height: 450 mm (lowered, 600 mm lifted)
Vehicle weight: 680 kg (empty)

Operating conditions
Temperature: 2°C to 40°C

Performance
Max travel speed: 1.8 m/s
Throughput: 40 pallet moves/hour
Navigation accuracy: ±10 mm

Safety: CE, EN ISO 3691-4 (driverless industrial trucks), ISO 13849-1 PLd

Communication: Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac), PROFINET (for fleet manager), REST API

Maintenance: Annual inspection plus 5,000-hour drive unit service
Warranty: 3 years

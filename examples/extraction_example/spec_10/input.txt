SPEC SHEET: WrapLine WL-500 Horizontal Flow Wrapper

Built by PackFlow Automation GmbH
Reference: PFA-WL500-MK3
Year of build: 2023

Intended use: The WL-500 is a HFFS (Horizontal Form Fill Seal) machine for primary packaging of baked goods, confectionery, and snack foods in the food processing industry. It handles products up to 500 mm in length.

Electrical specifications
  Supply voltage: 400 V, 3 phases, 50 Hz
  Power: 12 kW

Machine dimensions
  2,800 mm long × 1,100 mm wide × 1,700 mm tall

Operating environment
  Temperature: 10 °C min, 38 °C max
  Humidity: max 80%, non-condensing

Performance characteristics
  Film feed speed: up to 60 m/min
  Pack rate: 250 packs per minute
  Accuracy (seal jaw position): ±0.5 mm
  Noise level: 73 dB(A)

Certifications
  CE, EN 415-1 (packaging machine safety), EHEDG certified (hygienic design)

Communications
  EtherCAT, PROFINET, OPC UA

Warranty: 1 year standard

HydroForce HF-200 Centrifugal Process Pump

Technical Bulletin - Rev. 4

Maker: AquaPump Engineering Ltd.
Catalogue no.: APE-HF200-SS316
Year of mfg: 2023

Application: The HF-200 is a single-stage, end-suction centrifugal pump for chemical processing and water treatment applications. Wetted parts are constructed from 316L stainless steel for corrosion resistance.

Electrical
  380V 3-phase 50Hz supply
  Motor rated at 22 kW (IE3 efficiency)

Size
  Pump + baseplate: 1,450 mm (L) × 650 mm (W) × 780 mm (H)
  Dry weight: 385 kg

Temperature limits
  Process fluid temperature: -10°C to +120°C
  Ambient: 5°C minimum, 45°C maximum

Performance
  Impeller speed: 2,950 RPM
  Flow rate: 200 m³/h at 35m head
  
Certified to CE, EN ISO 5199 (technical specifications for centrifugal pumps), ATEX 2014/34/EU (Zone 2)

Service: Mechanical seal inspection every 6,000 hours
Warranty: 18 months from delivery

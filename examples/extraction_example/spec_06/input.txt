SPECIFICATION SUMMARY

Equipment: OverHead OHC-50 Double Girder Bridge Crane
Produced by: LiftCore Crane Systems Ltd.
Item code: LCS-OHC50-DG
Date of manufacture: 2022

The OHC-50 is a 50-tonne overhead bridge crane for heavy steel fabrication workshops. It has a span of 22 m with a lift height of 12 m.

Electrical Info
Supply: 400 V / three-phase / 50 Hz
Motor power (total): 45 kW (hoist 30 kW + travel 15 kW)

Dimensions
Bridge span: 22,000 mm
Overall width (including end carriages): 3,500 mm
Height above rail: 2,200 mm
Crane dead weight: 32 tonnes

Operating environment
Temperature: -10°C — +50°C

Performance
Hoist speed: 8 m/min
Travel speed: 40 m/min
Noise during operation: 74 dB(A)

Safety
CE marked per Machinery Directive
EN 15011 (cranes — bridge and gantry cranes)
EN 13135 (cranes — safety — design)

Service every 2,500 hours. Warranty 2 years on structural, 1 year on electrical.

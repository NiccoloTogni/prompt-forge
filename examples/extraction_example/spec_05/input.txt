InjectorMax IM-850T | Injection Molding Machine
================================================
Mfg: PlastiForce Technologies Inc.
P/N: PFT-IM850T-S
Built: 2024

Purpose-built for high-volume thermoplastic part production in the consumer electronics sector. The IM-850T offers 850 tonnes of clamping force with a shot weight up to 4,200 grams (PS equivalent).

ELECTRICAL DATA
Supply: 460 V / 3-ph / 60 Hz
Power consumption: 92 kW

SIZE & WEIGHT
The machine measures 680 cm (L) × 210 cm (W) × 250 cm (H).
Weight: 28 metric tons.

AMBIENT CONDITIONS
Operating temperature range: 50 °F to 104 °F
Max humidity: 70%

OUTPUT METRICS
- Screw speed up to 250 RPM
- Output rate: 120 shots/hour
- Part tolerance: ±0.03 mm
- Noise level: 76 dBA

COMPLIANCE
UL Listed, CE, EN 201 (safety of injection moulding machines), ANSI/NFPA 79

COMMUNICATIONS: EUROMAP 77 (OPC UA), CC-Link IE

SERVICING: Every 5,000 hours. WARRANTY: 1 year parts, 2 years labor.
